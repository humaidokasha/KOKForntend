Let us start…

Now you can start with first module “the danger centre”. This program is 6-weeks long. Each module will last for one week. However, the module “relationships” can be accessed at any time. Please fill the stress questionnaire at the start of each module. This will help you monitor your progress. At the end of every module, you will be advised to practice what you have learned. You will receive daily email reminders. Try to follow this program along with a family member or a friend. Please remember it takes time to learn new ways of thinking and to build new habits. The more you practice, the faster your will see the change.
